Fluid Simulation Shader

This simulation solves the Navier-Stokes equations for incompressible fluids in a GPU fragment shader. I implemented no-slip boundary conditions at the borders to keep the fluid contained within the bounds of the screen. To increase performance, I solved for the velocity vector field of the fluid at a lower resolution than I used to compute the visualization of fluid flow; I used bilinear interpolation to smooth out artifacts caused by this speedup. I've also added 160,000 Lagrangian particles on top of the simulation - these particles are rendered using threejs, but their positions are computed on the GPU.
